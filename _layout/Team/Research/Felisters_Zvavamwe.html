Felisters Zvavamwe (PhD Student)

Hi, I'm Felisters. I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Metallurgical Engineering from the University of Zimbabwe in 2015, then pursued a Master of Science in Metallurgical and Materials Engineering at Marmara University, Turkey, in November 2019. After completing my master's studies, I joined the University of Zimbabwe and worked as an instructor until December 2022, before moving to Canada to start my doctoral studies in January 2023.

I am working on the design of medium manganese steels, which are 3rd-generation Advanced High Strength Steels (AHSS) for automotive applications. I am also investigating the effect of microalloying these steels with vanadium and molybdenum on the equilibrium phases present.
