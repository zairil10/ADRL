Marvin Tolentino (PhD Student)

As a graduate student pursuing a PhD in Mechanical Engineering at the University of New Brunswick, I am currently developing and characterizing refractory high-entropy alloys produced via high-energy mechanical alloying, for use in coating applications using the cold spray additive manufacturing process.

Before pursuing my PhD, I was a Science Research Specialist in the Department of Science and Technology, working on materials development and characterization projects with the Advanced Manufacturing Center in the Philippines. My interest in research and development began after completing both my bachelor's and master's degrees in chemical engineering from Mapúa University, where I also worked as a college instructor teaching fundamental chemistry, science, and technology courses.
