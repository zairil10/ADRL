Hanna Regidor (PhD Student)

I obtained both my BSc. and MSc. degrees in Metallurgical Engineering at the University of the Philippines – Diliman. Having a passion for teaching and research, I became a faculty member of the same university after my MSc. and taught undergraduate metallurgical engineering subjects for 4 years. I am now pursuing a doctoral degree at the University of New Brunswick, where my research focuses on the characterization and modelling of the static and dynamic behavior of additively manufactured Ti64 alloy at elevated temperatures.
